Romeo L. Canda

Born on December 23rd in Mexico, Pampanga. Romeo L. Canda. aka "Romy" is considered as one of the finest realist painters in the country today. Romy's near hyper realist rendition in his paintings can be atrributed to decades of dedication to his art. He took up his Fine Arts degree at the prestigious University of Sto. Tomas School of Fine Arts. He also took up vocational course in photography that gave him edge among other artists.
